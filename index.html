How to play

Pokemon Epic BATTLE is a 2 players in turns game.

Each squares that a player move on belongs to this one. Then, the other player can't move on this square.

Each pokemon can use 1 common basic attack and 2 unique special attack.

Attacks can't be use every time, you have to wait some turns before use them

Bonuses appears randomly on the map and can be use to reduce attack's cool down

The winner is the one who have most square when all the map is covered.

Each players can also trap the other to win.

Controls

Player 1 : ZQSD and R to use special attack and E to use basic attack

Player 2 : Arrow Keys and 2 to use special attack and 1to use basic attack
